HOW TO PLAY: GUESS THE WORD

Guess the Word is an emoji puzzle game. You see two emojis, put their meanings together, and guess the word or phrase.

EXAMPLE
Fire + Dog = Hot Dog
Star + Fish = Starfish
Sun + Flower = Sunflower

GOAL
Score as many points as possible before you run out of lives.

HOW EACH ROUND WORKS
1. Look at the two emoji clues.
2. Type the answer and press Guess.
3. You can also tap one of the quick-answer buttons.
4. In Normal Mode, all hints and quick answers are open right away.
5. In Hard Mode, reveal hints to unlock more help.
6. Correct answers move you to the next round.
7. Wrong answers or time-outs cost 1 life.

SCORING
Fast answers score more.
Long streaks add bonus points.
Harder rounds add a small difficulty bonus.
In Hard Mode, revealing hints lowers the points you can earn that round.

LIVES AND SKIPS
You start with 3 lives.
You start with 3 skips.
Skipping reveals the answer, moves to a new puzzle, and resets your streak.

MODES
Chill Mode gives you more time.
Blitz Mode gives you less time and more pressure.
Hard Mode hides hints until you reveal them.
In Hard Mode, quick-answer buttons unlock after hint 2.
Daily Mix gives everyone the same seeded run for the day.
Shuffle Round swaps in a fresh puzzle.

DIFFICULTY
The game gets harder the longer you survive.
The timer gets shorter.
The answer grid adds more choices.
Later rounds hide the category.

SOUNDS AND MUSIC
Buttons have click sounds.
Correct answers play a success sound and confetti.
Wrong answers play a miss sound.
Use Sound to mute effects.
Use Music to toggle background music.

WHY IT STAYS FRESH
The game uses a curated compound-word answer bank and emoji variation engine, but active rounds now pick the clearest emoji pair first so clues feel fair.
Answers do not repeat in the same run until the pool cycles.
